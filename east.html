East Coast Hip Hop

East Coast is known for aggressive beats and complex lyricism.

Originating in the Bronx, NY, East Coast hip hop emphasizes lyricism, boom bap beats, and jazz/funk influences. Artists like Nas, Biggie, and A Tribe Called Quest are major figures. The production often focuses on sampled drums, and the lyrical content tends to be reflective, political, or street-centric.
